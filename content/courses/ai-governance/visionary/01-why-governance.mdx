## Why AI Governance?

AI systems make decisions that affect people's lives -- hiring, lending, healthcare, criminal justice. Without <JargonTip term="AI governance">governance</JargonTip>, these systems can perpetuate bias, lack transparency, and cause real harm.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🚦" title="Think of it like traffic laws">Roads without traffic laws would be dangerous chaos. AI without governance is similar -- powerful technology that needs guardrails to ensure it benefits everyone safely.</AnalogyBox>
