## ReAct: Reason + Act

The most fundamental agentic pattern. The <JargonTip term="ReAct">ReAct</JargonTip> loop: the model **thinks** about what to do, **acts** (calls a tool), **observes** the result, then **thinks** again.

<CodeBlock>
