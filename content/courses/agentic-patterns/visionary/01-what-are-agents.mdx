## What Are Agentic Patterns?

<AnalogyBox emoji="📖" title="Recipes for AI Agents">Agentic patterns are like recipes in a cookbook -- proven step-by-step approaches for building AI agents that can think, act, and iterate. Just as a chef picks the right recipe for the occasion, you pick the right pattern for your use case.</AnalogyBox>

Agentic AI goes beyond single-shot prompting. An <JargonTip term="agent">agent</JargonTip> **observes**, **reasons**, **acts**, and **iterates** -- using tools, checking results, and adjusting its approach.

- **🔄 ReAct** -- Reason + Act: think step-by-step, call tools, observe results, repeat
- **📋 Plan-and-Execute** -- Create a plan upfront, then execute each step systematically
- **🪞 Reflection** -- Generate, critique, revise -- self-improvement through iteration
- **🔧 Tool-Use Loops** -- Iteratively call tools until the task is complete or a limit is reached
