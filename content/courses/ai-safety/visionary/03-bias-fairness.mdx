## Bias & Fairness

AI systems inherit and amplify biases present in their training data. Understanding bias types is the first step to building <JargonTip term="fairness">fairer</JargonTip> systems.

- **Training Data Bias** — Biases baked into the data the model learned from (historical discrimination, underrepresentation)
- **Selection Bias** — Non-representative sampling — training on English-only data, or only data from certain demographics
- **Automation Bias** — Humans over-trusting AI decisions, not questioning AI-generated output
- **Representation Bias** — Some groups are underrepresented in training data, leading to worse performance for those groups
