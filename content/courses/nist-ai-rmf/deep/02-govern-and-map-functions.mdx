## Govern & Map Functions



### GOVERN: The Foundation

The GOVERN function establishes organizational AI governance infrastructure. It answers: *Who is accountable? What policies exist? How is AI risk culture cultivated?*

<CodeBlock language="text" label="GOVERN Subcategories" code={`GOVERN 1: Policies, processes, procedures
GOVERN 2: Accountability structures  
GOVERN 3: Workforce diversity & AI literacy
GOVERN 4: Organizational risk culture
GOVERN 5: Stakeholder engagement
GOVERN 6: AI system lifecycle governance`}/>

### MAP: Scoping the AI Landscape

MAP is about understanding context before measuring anything. What AI systems exist? Who uses them? What could go wrong?

<AnalogyBox emoji="🗺️" title="Data Discovery Before Data Quality">
In MDM, you don't start with data quality rules — you start with data discovery. What systems exist? What data flows where? Who owns what? MAP is the AI equivalent: before you measure AI risk, you map the AI landscape.
</AnalogyBox>
