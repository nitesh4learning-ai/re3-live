## NIST AI RMF Overview



The NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) provides a structured approach to managing AI risks. Released in January 2023, it's voluntary but rapidly becoming the de facto standard for US enterprises.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🏛️" title="NIST = Building Codes for AI">
Building codes don't tell you what to build — they tell you how to build safely. NIST AI RMF does the same for AI: it doesn't dictate what models to use, but how to manage the risks of using them. Just as building inspectors verify compliance, AI governance teams verify AI systems meet the framework's guidelines.
</AnalogyBox>
