## Measure & Manage Functions



### MEASURE: Quantifying AI Risk

MEASURE answers: *How do we know if our AI systems are performing safely and effectively?*

<ComparisonTable title="AI Risk Metrics" columns={['Metric Type','Example','Measurement Method','Frequency']} rows={[
  ['Accuracy','Model prediction accuracy','Test set evaluation','Monthly'],

  ['Robustness','Performance under adversarial inputs','Red team testing','Semi-annual'],
  ['Explainability','Feature importance transparency','SHAP/LIME analysis','Per-model'],

### MANAGE: Acting on Findings

MANAGE closes the loop: prioritize risks identified by MEASURE, implement mitigations, monitor effectiveness, respond to incidents.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🔧" title="CAPA in Quality Management">
If you've worked with CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Action) in quality management, MANAGE follows the same pattern: identify the issue (from MEASURE), determine root cause, implement corrective action, verify effectiveness, prevent recurrence. NIST just applies this to AI risk.
</AnalogyBox>
