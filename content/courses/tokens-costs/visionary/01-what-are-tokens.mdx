## What Are Tokens?

<AnalogyBox emoji={'D83EDE99'} title="Arcade Coins">{`Tokens are like coins in an arcade — different machines (models) have different prices per play. And some words cost more coins than others! "The" is 1 token, but "antidisestablishmentarianism" might be 6+ tokens.`}</AnalogyBox>

<JargonTip term="token">Tokens</JargonTip> are **subword units**, not words. LLMs break text into tokens using <JargonTip term="BPE">BPE</JargonTip> (Byte-Pair Encoding). Common words like "the" are 1 token, but uncommon words get split into pieces.

  <div style={{fontSize:11,color:GIM.mutedText}}>{w.tokens} token{w.tokens>1?'s':''}</div>
</div>)}</div>

<ExpandableSection title="Why Tokens Matter" icon={'\u26A1'}>
  - They determine cost -- you pay per token, not per word
  - They set context window limits -- 128K tokens \u2248 96K words for English
  - Different languages tokenize differently -- Chinese/Japanese use more tokens per concept
  - Code is token-heavy -- variable names, syntax, indentation all consume tokens
</ExpandableSection>
