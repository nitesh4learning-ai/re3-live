## Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

<JargonTip term="chain-of-thought">Chain-of-Thought</JargonTip> (CoT) prompting asks the model to **show its reasoning step-by-step** before giving a final answer. This dramatically improves accuracy on complex tasks like math, logic, and multi-step analysis.

The magic phrase? Simply adding **"Let's think step by step"** to your prompt can improve accuracy by 20-40% on reasoning tasks.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🧩" title="Think of it like showing your work">In math class, showing your work helps catch errors. CoT does the same for AI -- by reasoning step-by-step, the model is less likely to jump to an incorrect conclusion.</AnalogyBox>

]}/>

<ExpandableSection title="Structured Output Prompting" icon="📋">

Beyond CoT, you can control the exact output format:

**JSON mode:** "Respond with valid JSON matching this schema: {'{'}name, score, reasoning{'}'}"

**Markdown:** "Format your response as a markdown table with columns: Feature, Pro, Con"

**XML tags:** "Wrap your analysis in {'<analysis>'} tags and your answer in {'<answer>'} tags"

Structured outputs are especially useful when the AI's response will be parsed by code downstream.

</ExpandableSection>
