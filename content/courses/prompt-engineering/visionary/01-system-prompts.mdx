## System Prompts

A <JargonTip term="system prompt">system prompt</JargonTip> is a hidden instruction given to the AI before any user input. It defines the AI's **role**, **personality**, **constraints**, and **output format**. Think of it as the "job description" the AI reads before starting work.

Well-crafted system prompts are the single most impactful way to control AI behavior consistently across all interactions.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🎬" title="Think of it like a director's brief">A director gives actors their role, motivation, and boundaries before filming begins. The system prompt does the same for the AI -- setting expectations before any user interaction.</AnalogyBox>

<CodeBlock>
{`Role:        "You are a senior financial analyst..."
Constraints: "Never provide specific investment advice."
Format:      "Respond in bullet points with citations."
Tone:        "Be professional but approachable."
Examples:    "When asked about trends, structure as..."

Full example:
"You are a senior financial analyst specializing in market trends.
Provide data-driven insights in bullet-point format.
Always cite your reasoning. Never give specific buy/sell advice.
If uncertain, say 'I don't have enough data to assess this.'"`}
</CodeBlock>
