## The Black Box Problem

Only 20% of organizations have deployed AI into production. The blocker isn't model accuracy — it's inability to explain decisions to compliance, auditors, and executives. Three levels: model-level (global), prediction-level (local), system-level (operational).

<AnalogyBox emoji="📊" title="Data Lineage for AI">
AI explainability is data lineage for predictions: what training data shaped this output, what features drove this decision, what intermediate steps occurred?
