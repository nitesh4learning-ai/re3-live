## LIME and Beyond

LIME explains individual predictions by building simple local surrogate models. Beyond SHAP/LIME: attention maps (which tokens mattered?), counterfactual explanations (what would change the outcome?), TCAV (concept-level explanations), and agent execution traces (full decision chain logging).
