## Beyond Naive RAG

Naive RAG is a pipeline: Query → Retrieve → Generate. It fails when retrieval quality is poor — the LLM hallucinates from irrelevant documents.

<AnalogyBox emoji="📚" title="Library Research vs Google Search">
Naive RAG is Googling and reading the first result. Agentic RAG is a research librarian: search, evaluate sources, try different terms if results are poor, and only synthesize when confident in quality.
</AnalogyBox>

Agentic RAG adds self-correction loops: grade documents, rewrite queries, check for hallucinations. It's the LangGraph cyclic graph pattern applied to retrieval.
