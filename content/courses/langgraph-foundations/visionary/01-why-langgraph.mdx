## Why LangGraph?

<JargonTip term="LangGraph">LangGraph</JargonTip> is a framework for building AI agents that can **loop, retry, and self-correct** — things regular LangChain chains can't do.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🗺️" title="GPS vs Conveyor Belt">
LangChain is a conveyor belt: A → B → C, fixed path. LangGraph is GPS navigation: it continuously evaluates conditions and reroutes when needed. That's the power of cyclic graphs.
</AnalogyBox>

### Three Building Blocks

Every LangGraph app has **State** (shared memory), **Nodes** (actions), and **Edges** (flow control). Conditional edges let the graph make decisions — routing to different nodes based on what's in state.
