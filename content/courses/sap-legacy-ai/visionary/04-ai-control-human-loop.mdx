## Agentic SAP with Guardrails

Five autonomy levels from manual (Level 0) to fully autonomous (Level 4). Most enterprises should target Level 3: AI executes within rules, humans monitor and intervene on exceptions. Two human gates in the modernization pipeline: validate AI's code understanding, then approve proposed changes before deployment.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🏭" title="Change Advisory Board for AI">
In ITIL, a CAB reviews changes before production. AI modernization follows the same governance: AI proposes, humans review, tests verify, transport system deploys. AI accelerates work; humans ensure safety.
