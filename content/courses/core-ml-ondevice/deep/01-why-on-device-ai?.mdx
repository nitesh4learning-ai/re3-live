## Why On-Device AI?



### Why On-Device AI Matters

Cloud AI has three costs: money (API calls), latency (network round-trips), and privacy (data leaves the device). On-device inference eliminates all three for suitable workloads.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🧠" title="Edge Computing = Local Data Processing">
On-device AI is to cloud AI what edge computing is to cloud computing in enterprise architecture. At McKesson, you wouldn't send every barcode scan to a central server for validation — the warehouse scanner validates locally. Similarly, SnapSense doesn't need to send every image to the cloud for basic category detection.
</AnalogyBox>
