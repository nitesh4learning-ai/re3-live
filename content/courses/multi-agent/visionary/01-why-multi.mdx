## Why Multi-Agent?

A single AI <JargonTip term="agent">agent</JargonTip> can handle simple tasks. But for complex problems requiring diverse expertise, multiple perspectives, or sequential workflows, **<JargonTip term="multi-agent">multi-agent</JargonTip> systems** dramatically outperform single agents.

  <AnalogyBox emoji="👥" title="Think of it like a specialized team">One person doing research, writing, editing, and fact-checking will produce lower quality than a team with a researcher, writer, editor, and fact-checker -- each focused on what they do best.</AnalogyBox>
