## EU AI Act Overview



The EU AI Act is the world's first comprehensive AI regulation. Entering enforcement in stages (August 2024 → August 2027), it classifies AI systems into four risk tiers and imposes specific obligations on each.

<AnalogyBox emoji="🇪🇺" title="GDPR for AI">
The EU AI Act is to AI what GDPR was to data privacy — a comprehensive regulation that will ripple globally. Just as GDPR forced every company handling EU citizen data to comply, the AI Act will force every company deploying AI in the EU to classify their systems and meet tier-specific requirements.
</AnalogyBox>
