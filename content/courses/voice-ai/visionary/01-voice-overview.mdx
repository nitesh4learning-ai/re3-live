## Voice AI & Conversational Agents

Voice AI transforms how humans interact with machines \u2014 from contact centers to personal assistants to accessibility tools. The core pipeline involves <JargonTip term="STT">STT</JargonTip> (speech-to-text), LLM processing, and <JargonTip term="TTS">TTS</JargonTip> (text-to-speech), with <JargonTip term="NER">NER</JargonTip> for extracting entities like names and dates from spoken input.

- **STT** -- Speech-to-Text: convert audio to text (Whisper, Deepgram)
- **NLU** -- Natural Language Understanding: interpret intent from text
- **TTS** -- Text-to-Speech: generate natural-sounding voice (ElevenLabs, OpenAI TTS)
