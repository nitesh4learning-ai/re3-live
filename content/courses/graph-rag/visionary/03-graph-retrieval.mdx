## Graph-Enhanced Retrieval

Standard <JargonTip term="RAG">RAG</JargonTip> finds documents similar to your query. **<JargonTip term="GraphRAG">Graph RAG</JargonTip>** enhances this by following relationships in the knowledge graph to find connected information that pure vector search would miss.

  <AnalogyBox emoji="🔍" title="Vector search vs Graph search">Vector search: "Find documents about Einstein" → finds articles mentioning Einstein. Graph search: Start at Einstein node, follow relationships → discovers connected concepts like Relativity, Nobel Prize, Quantum Mechanics.</AnalogyBox>
