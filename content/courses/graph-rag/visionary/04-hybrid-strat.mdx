## Hybrid Search Strategies

The most powerful retrieval systems combine multiple strategies: **vector search** for semantic similarity, **keyword search** for exact matches, and **graph traversal** for relationship discovery. This combination is known as <JargonTip term="hybrid search">hybrid search</JargonTip>.



| Method | Finds | Misses |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Vector Search | Semantically similar content | Exact terms, connected entities |
| Keyword Search | Exact term matches | Synonyms, related concepts |
| Graph Traversal | Entity relationships, multi-hop paths | Unstructured content |
| Hybrid (all three) | Comprehensive results | (Most complete coverage) |
