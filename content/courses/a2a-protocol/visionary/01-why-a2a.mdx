## Why A2A?

<JargonTip term="MCP">MCP</JargonTip> connects AI to **tools**. But what about connecting AI to **other AI <JargonTip term="agent">agents</JargonTip>**? The **<JargonTip term="A2A">Agent-to-Agent (A2A)</JargonTip>** protocol enables AI agents to discover each other, negotiate capabilities, and collaborate on tasks.

<AnalogyBox emoji="📞" title="MCP vs A2A: Phone analogy">MCP is like calling a service (pizza delivery, bank). A2A is like calling a colleague to collaborate on a project. Different relationship, different protocol.</AnalogyBox>

| Aspect | MCP | A2A |
|--------|-----|-----|
| **Connects** | AI to Tools/Data | AI to AI Agents |
| **Pattern** | Client-Server | Peer-to-Peer |
| **Communication** | Tool calls | Task delegation |
| **Discovery** | Server exposes tools | Agent Cards advertise capabilities |
