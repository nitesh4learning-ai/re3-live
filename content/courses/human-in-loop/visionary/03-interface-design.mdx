## Designing HITL Interfaces

<ExpandableSection title="Good HITL Design Principles" icon="💡" defaultOpen={true}>

- Show AI reasoning -- humans need explainability to understand WHY the AI made a decision
- Make approval fast -- one-click approve/reject with editable drafts
- Provide context -- show relevant history, similar past decisions, confidence level
- Track overrides -- log when humans change AI decisions for future improvement via guardrails
- Set SLAs -- escalated items need response time targets to avoid bottlenecks

</ExpandableSection>
