## Why Human-in-the-Loop?

<AnalogyBox emoji="✈️" title="The Autopilot Analogy">AI with human-in-the-loop is like a plane on autopilot -- it handles routine operations automatically, but the pilot takes over for takeoff, landing, and turbulence. The key is knowing when to hand off control.</AnalogyBox>

Not every AI decision should be automated. <JargonTip term="HITL">Human-in-the-loop (HITL)</JargonTip> patterns keep humans in control of **high-stakes**, **ambiguous**, or **novel** decisions while automating the routine.

- **✅ Approval Gates** -- AI proposes, human approves before action is taken
- **🚨 Escalation** -- AI handles routine cases, uses escalation for edge cases to humans
- **📊 Confidence Thresholds** -- Auto-act when confident, ask for help when uncertain
- **🔄 Feedback Loops** -- Human corrections improve the AI over time
