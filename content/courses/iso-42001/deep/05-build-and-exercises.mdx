### Week 6 Build: AIMS Policy Document

Draft an AI Management System policy document for an enterprise context.

**Deliverables:**
1. AIMS policy document with: scope, AI risk register template, roles/responsibilities matrix, KPIs
2. AI impact assessment template (based on ISO 42001 Clause 8)
3. Gap analysis: current organization state vs ISO 42001 requirements
4. Certification readiness assessment
